Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes, you can change your subscription plan directly from the app. When upgrading, the new features will be available immediately. Our flexible subscription system allows you to adjust your plan as your business grows, ensuring you always have access to the features you need.

How does the yearly discount work?

When you choose yearly billing, you get a 20% discount compared to monthly billing. This means you'll save significantly while enjoying all the features for a full year. The discount is automatically applied when selecting the annual plan, and you'll see the total discounted price before confirming your subscription.

What payment methods are accepted?

All payments are processed through the App Store using your Apple ID payment method. This includes credit and debit cards, PayPal (where available), Apple Pay, and any other payment methods supported by your local App Store. Your subscription will be automatically renewed using the same payment method unless cancelled.
